# Inaka (田舎) Rural Property Websites Research Report

**Research Date:** March 16, 2026  
**Focus:** Specialized rural property websites for Akiya Japan app - focusing on under ¥10M properties, kominka, and rural living support sites.

---

## 1. Major Aggregator Sites (全国規模のポータルサイト)

### 1.1 Akiya Japan (アキヤジャパン)
- **URL:** https://www.akiyajapan.com/
- **Property Count:** 168,135 active listings
- **Coverage:** 47 prefectures nationwide
- **Average Price:** $179,359 (~¥27M)
- **Properties Under $50K:** 44,108 listings
- **Listing Format:** Web-based search with filters
- **Languages:** English/Japanese bilingual
- **Unique Features:**
  - English platform targeting international buyers
  - Partner agent Teritoru for bilingual support
  - 2020 launch, established player
  - Property types: Akiya Bank, Apartment, Business, Cottage, Duplex, Farmhouse, Detached, Land, New construction, Townhouse, Kominka, Warehouse
- **Price Range for Under ¥10M:** Many properties available, especially in rural areas

- **Buy:** 9,697 properties
- **Rent:** 1,340 properties

- **Unique Features:**
  - Official akiya bank aggregator

    - 温泉地域の物件特集 (Hot spring areas)
    - ペットと暮らせる賃貸特集 (Pet-friendly)
  - Regional guides and migration support info
- **Price Range:** Many properties under ¥10M, especially in rural regions

### 1.3 LIFULL HOMES Akiya Bank

## 2. 田舎暮らし (Inaka Living) Specialized Sites

### 2.1 Inaka Net (田舎ねっと.日本)
- **URL:** https://www.inakanet.jp/
- **Type:** Rural living property portal
- **Property Count:** Not disclosed, but regularly updated
- **Listing Format:** Custom CGI-based database
- **Unique Features:**
  - Daily updated listings
  - Property types: 古民家 (kominka), ログハウス (log houses), 別荘 (second homes), 戸建 (detached), 土地 (land)
  - Special focus on: 家庭菜園 (vegetable gardens), 畑 (farmland), 自給自足 (self-sufficiency)
  - Regional popularity rankings (人気物件ランキング)
  - Regional versions for all of Japan
  - Special collections for kominka and log houses
  - Free guidebooks for rural living
- **Price Range:** Many properties ¥2M-¥15M, focus on affordable rural living
- **Sample Listings:**
  - 岡山 新見市 古民家 380万円
  - 三重 大台町 戸建 530万円
  - 奈良 宇陀市 古民家 480万円

## 3. 古民家再生 (Kominka Revival) Sites

### 3.1 Kominka Sumairu (古民家住まいる)
- **URL:** http://kominka.net
- **Type:** Kominka matching site
- **Affiliation:** General incorporated association National Kominka Revival Association (全国古民家再生協会)
- **Unique Features:**
  - Specialized kominka filters (大黒柱, 蔵付き)
  - Properties include "古民家再生総合調査報告書" or "木造住宅簡易鑑定書"
  - Only registered real estate companies with kominka appraisers can list
  - Limited but vetted listings
  - Higher trust factor due to certification requirements

### 3.2 Nihon Minka Saisei Kyokai (日本民家再生協会)
- **URL:** https://minka.or.jp/minkabank
- **Type:** NPO Minka Bank
- **Unique Features:**
  - Traditional minka (民家) focused
  - Relocation/reconstruction premise
  - Building itself is free (no cost)
  - Separate fees for information, dismantling, transport
  - Authentic traditional buildings
  - Non-profit operation

### 3.3 Kominkai.net (古民家探訪)
- **URL:** https://kominkai.net/
- **Type:** Blog/resource site about kominka living
- **Content:** Lists 12 recommended sites for finding kominka
- **Unique Features:**
  - Educational content about kominka
  - Links to various specialized sites
  - Community for kominka enthusiasts

## 6. Municipal Akiya Banks (自治体空き家バンク)

### Characteristics:
- **URL Pattern:** Search "[Region name] 空き家バンク"
- **Examples:**
  - Fukuoka: https://akiyabank.f-takken.com
  - Tokyo: https://www.juutakuseisaku.metro.tokyo.lg.jp/akiya/bank
- **Property Count:** Varies by municipality (few to hundreds)
- **Listing Format:** Usually simple HTML, sometimes PDF
- **Unique Features:**
  - Direct owner-to-buyer matching possible
  - Subsidy programs often available
  - No brokerage fees in some cases
  - Limited information (often only 2-3 photos)
  - Property condition often unclear
  - Must visit in person to assess

## 7. Price Range Analysis (Under ¥10M Properties)

### Very Low (¥0 - ¥1M)
- Sources: Municipal akiya banks, Sumai Akiya, Jimoty
- Condition: Often requires significant renovation
- Examples: ¥500K-¥1M for older homes in rural areas

### Affordable (¥1M - ¥5M)
- Sources: All major sites
- Condition: Variable, some move-in ready, some need work
- Examples: ¥2M-¥3M for kominka with land

### Moderate (¥5M - ¥10M)
- Sources: All sites
- Condition: Generally better condition, some renovated
- Examples: ¥5M-¥8M for renovated kominka or homes with large land

## 8. Data Format Summary

### API Availability
- **Limited:** Most sites use HTML scraping only
- **Possible JSON/API:** Athome, LIFULL HOMES, SUUMO (major portals may have APIs for partners)

### HTML Structure
- **Structured:** Athome, Akiya Japan, Homes.co.jp
- **Semi-structured:** Sumai Akiya, Inaka Net
- **Basic:** Municipal akiya banks (variable quality)

### Listing Information Typically Available
- Price
- Location (prefecture, city, sometimes address)
- Property type
- Land area
- Building area
- Number of rooms
- Year built
- Photos (variable quality/quantity)
- Description
- Contact information

## 9. Recommendations for Akiya Japan App

### Priority Sources for Scraping/API Integration:
1. **Athome Akiya Bank** - Largest official aggregation, good structure
2. **Akiya Japan** - English content, international focus
3. **Sumai Akiya** - Affordable properties, unique features
4. **Inaka Net** - Rural living focus, kominka specializations
5. **Rakuen Shinshu** - Model for regional portals

### Niche Sources for Specific Features:
- **Kominka Sumairu** - Certified kominka
- **Minka Saisei Kyokai** - Traditional minka (relocation)
- **Ieichiba/Jimoty** - Direct owner listings (price comparison)

### Content Strategy:
- Focus on under ¥10M properties (highest demand)
- Highlight unique features (farmland, hot springs, traditional elements)
- Include subsidy/support information by region
- Provide disaster hazard information (following Rakuen Shinshu model)

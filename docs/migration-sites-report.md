- **Contact:** Available via inquiry form, phone
- **Data Format:** Web-based listings with detailed property pages
- **Special Features:** VR tours, password-protected new listings for subscribers
- **English Support:** Limited (Japanese primary)

## 2. 九州田舎不動産 (Kyushu Inaka Real Estate)

### Overview
- **Website:** http://inakafudousan.jp
- **Company:** 田舎不動産株式会社 (Inaka Fudosan Co., Ltd.)
- **Location:** Fukuoka Prefecture, Kurate District, Kotake Town
- **Focus:** Kyushu region specialist (Fukuoka, Oita, Kumamoto, etc.)
- **Established:** 2005

### Property Counts & Regional Coverage
- **Total Properties:** ~50+ active listings (curated, boutique approach)
- **Coverage:** Kyushu region only
  - **Fukuoka Prefecture:** Primary focus (Ukiha City, Asakura City, etc.)
  - **Oita Prefecture:** Strong presence (Hita City, Amagase Town - onsen/hot spring properties)
  - **Kumamoto Prefecture:** Some listings (Aso District, Minamioguni Town)
  - **Other Kyushu prefectures:** Limited but available

### Support Services Offered
- **Property Matching:** Personalized consultation for country living
- **Onsen Specialist:** Hot spring property expertise
- **Renovation Support:** "古民家再生" (Kominka restoration services)
- **Property Viewing:** On-site visit application system
- **Blog:** Regular updates on properties and rural life
- **Member System:** ID/PW based member area
- **Antique Furnishings:** "古民具" (Traditional household goods) sales
- **Publication:** Author of "田舎物件はこうして探す" (How to Find Country Properties) - sold out

### Price Ranges
- **Low-end:** 300万円 (3 million yen / ~$20,000 USD) - e.g., Oita vacation homes with garden
- **Mid-range:** 600万円 - 1,000万円 (typical range)
- **High-end:** 1,200万円+ (e.g., farmhouse with land)
- **Sample Listings:**
  - Oita onsen vacation home: 366万円 - 950万円
  - Fukuoka kominka: 950万円 - 1,200万円
  - Kumamoto onsen property: 600万円

### Property Types
- Onsen/hot spring vacation homes (温泉付き売り別荘) - SPECIALTY
- Traditional kominka (古民家)
- Self-sufficient properties (自給自足物件)
- Properties with farmland (家庭菜園付き)
- Cafe/shop-suitable properties (カフェ向き)
- Rental properties (賃貸物件)

### Integration Potential
- **Medium-High** - Specialized regional focus, personal service
- **Contact:** Inquiry form available
- **Data Format:** HTML-based listings (older website structure)
- **Unique Value:** Onsen property specialist, renovation expertise
- **English Support:** Not available (Japanese only)

## 3. Regional Revitalization Portals

### 3.1 アットホーム 空き家バンク (At Home Akiya Bank)
- **Website:** https://www.akiya-athome.jp
- **Operator:** At Home Co., Ltd. (major real estate portal)
- **Properties:** 11,034 listings
- **Participating Municipalities:** 894 local governments
- **Coverage:** All 47 prefectures
- **Features:**
  - Municipal akiya bank aggregation
  - Theme-based search (under 1 million yen, kominka, pet-friendly, etc.)
  - Trial migration experiences (おためし移住)
  - Regional revitalization corps info (地域おこし協力隊)
  - Relocation consultation windows (移住相談窓口)

### 3.2 ニッポン移住・交流ナビ JOIN (Japan Migration & Exchange Navi JOIN)
- **Website:** https://www.iju-join.jp
- **Focus:** Migration support and regional exchange
- **Features:**
  - Akiya bank listings by region
  - Regional migration programs
  - Municipal support information
  - Trial living experiences (おためし移住)

### 3.3 Regional Revitalization Corps (地域おこし協力隊)
- **Program:** Government-sponsored regional revitalization
- **Support:** 
  - Salary: ~3 million yen/year
  - Assignment: 1-3 years in rural areas
  - Housing support included
- **Websites:** Various municipal portals

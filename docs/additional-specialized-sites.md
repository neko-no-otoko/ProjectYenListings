Beyond the major aggregators, many prefectures maintain their own akiya banks:

| Region | Portal URL | Notes |
|--------|------------|-------|
| Nagano (楽園信州) | https://rakuen-akiya.jp/ | 923 properties, trial housing program |

| Tokyo | https://www.juutakuseisaku.metro.tokyo.lg.jp/akiya/bank | Official Tokyo portal |
| Kyoto | Search "京都府 空き家バンク" | Prefectural listings |
| Hokkaido | Search "北海道 空き家バンク" | Cold climate focus |

### 3.2 Municipal Direct Listing Sites

Many smaller municipalities maintain simple HTML sites:

**URL Pattern:** `[city].akiyabank.jp` or `[prefecture]-[city].gov.jp/akiya`

**Characteristics:**
- Simple HTML, sometimes PDF listings
- Direct contact with municipal housing divisions
- Often include subsidy information
- Limited photos (2-3 typically)
- May require in-person visits

**Access Strategy:**
- Search "[Municipality name] 空き家バンク"
- Check prefectural akiya bank for links
- Contact municipal housing department directly

## 4. Kominka and Traditional Home Specialists

### 4.1 Kominka Sumairu (古民家住まいる)

**URL:** http://kominka.net

**Specialization:**
- Kominka (old folk houses) only
- Certified properties with inspection reports
- Members: National Kominka Revival Association (全国古民家再生協会)

**Unique Features:**
- "古民家再生総合調査報告書" or "木造住宅簡易鑑定書" included
- Only registered real estate companies with kominka appraisers
- Higher trust factor due to certification

### 4.2 Nihon Minka Saisei Kyokai (日本民家再生協会)

**URL:** https://minka.or.jp/minkabank

**Specialization:**
- Traditional minka (民家) focused
- Relocation/reconstruction premise
- Building itself is free (no cost)
- Separate fees for info, dismantling, transport

**Note:** Non-profit operation - authentic traditional buildings

### 4.3 Kominkai.net (古民家探訪)

**URL:** https://kominkai.net/

**Type:** Blog/resource site about kominka living

**Value:**
- Lists 12 recommended sites for finding kominka
- Educational content about kominka
- Community for kominka enthusiasts

## 5. Niche and Direct Owner Sites

### 5.1 Ieichiba (家いちば)

**URL:** https://www.ieichiba.com/

**Type:** Bulletin board style direct sales

**Features:**
- Direct owner-to-buyer matching
- Lower fees than traditional real estate
- Categories: 古民家, 離島, 温泉
- FSBO (For Sale By Owner) style

### 5.2 Jimoty (ジモティー)

**URL:** https://jmty.jp/

**Type:** Free classifieds (Craigslist-style)

**Features:**
- Personal sales possible
- Price negotiation flexibility
- Need for due diligence emphasized
- Good for unexpected deals

### 5.3 Sumai Akiya (Sumai空き家)

**URL:** https://akiya.sumai.biz/

**Stats:** 3,122 vacant properties

**Unique Categories:**
- Transfer-type lease (賃貸譲渡)
- Airbnb/short-term rental suitable
- Houses with hearths (囲炉裏)
- Houses with wood-fired baths (薪風呂)
- Houses with sunken hearths (掘りごたつ)

**Regional Breakdown:**
| Region | Properties |
|--------|------------|
| Hokkaido | 88 |
| Tohoku | 469 |
| Kanto | 190 |
| Chubu | 633 |
| Kinki | 566 |
| Chugoku | 450 |
| Shikoku | 205 |
| Kyushu | 483 |

### 5.4 Inaka Net (田舎ねっと.日本)

**URL:** https://www.inakanet.jp/

**Type:** Rural living property portal

**Features:**
- Daily updated listings
- Property types: 古民家, ログハウス, 別荘, 戸建, 土地
- Special focus: 家庭菜園, 畑, 自給自足
- Regional versions for all of Japan

## 11. Recommendations for Integration

### Priority 1: High-Value Targets
1. **Municipal Akiya Banks**
   - Direct partnership opportunity
   - Often most affordable properties
   - Subsidy information included

2. **Sumai Akiya**
   - Good structure for scraping
   - Unique categories (薪風呂, 囲炉裏)
   - ~3,000 properties

### Priority 2: Specialized Niches
3. **Kominka Sumairu**
   - Certified kominka only
   - Higher trust/quality
   - Premium market segment

4. **Inaka Net**
   - Daily updates
   - Rural lifestyle focus
   - Self-sufficiency angle

### Priority 3: Supplementary
5. **Direct Owner Sites (Ieichiba, Jimoty)**
   - Price comparison
   - FSBO opportunities
   - Requires more due diligence
